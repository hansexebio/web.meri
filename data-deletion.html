Data Deletion Options in Meri+ App

The Meri+ app allows users to delete their data at any time. Below are the steps to remove specific types of content directly from the app:

Posts

  • To delete an individual post, open your post, tap the three dots (⋮), and select Delete Post.
  • To delete all your posts, go to Settings > Posts, and tap Delete All Posts. Confirm your decision to permanently remove all your posts.

Comments

  • To delete a comment, go to the post, long-press your comment, and select Delete Comment. Confirm to remove it.

Stories

  • To delete a story, open your story, tap the three dots (⋮), and select Delete Story. Confirm to complete the deletion.

Opinions

  • To delete an opinion, go to the story, tap the comment icon to enter the opinions section, long-press your opinion, and select Delete Opinion. Confirm to remove it.

Chats

  • To leave and delete a chat, long-press the conversation and choose Leave Chat. If all members leave, the chat is permanently removed.

Messages

  • To delete a message, open the chat, long-press your message, and select Delete Message. Confirm the action to remove it.

Note: All deletion actions are final and cannot be undone. For privacy and security reasons, data deletions can only be performed from within the app.
