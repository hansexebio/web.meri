Delete Your Meri+ App Account

You may choose to permanently delete your Meri+ account and all related data directly from the app. This process is immediate and irreversible — once completed, your account cannot be restored.

To delete your account:

  • Open the Meri+ app and log in.
  • Go to the Profile tab and tap Settings.
  • Select the General section.
  • Tap on "Delete account", confirm, and follow the instructions.

Important information before deleting:

  • This action is permanent — your account and all data will be permanently erased.
  • Your posts, stories, chats, and personal information will be deleted.
  • Any items or features purchased using MeriCoins will not be refunded.
  • You must be logged in to perform this action. Guest users or unauthenticated requests cannot delete accounts.
  • For your safety, account deletion requests via email or website are not accepted to prevent identity fraud.
