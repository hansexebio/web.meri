Commitment to Child Safety

Our app is intended exclusively for users aged 16 and older. However, we maintain a strong commitment to preventing all forms of child sexual abuse and exploitation (CSEA).

As part of our safety policies, we declare the following:

  • We strictly prohibit any content involving child sexual abuse or exploitation.
  • We use both automated and manual systems to detect, report, and remove content or users who violate this policy.
  • We cooperate with relevant authorities whenever potential violations are identified.
  • We provide accessible reporting tools within the platform so users can safely report inappropriate content.
  • We regularly train our team to recognize and respond to possible cases of CSEA.

If you encounter any suspicious content or believe a violation of these policies has occurred, please contact us immediately at: hansexebio@gmail.com
